# Anthropic

## Overview
Anthropic PBC is an American artificial intelligence company headquartered in San Francisco, California, focused on AI safety research and the development of large language models (LLMs). Its flagship product line is the Claude series of LLMs. Anthropic operates as a public benefit corporation and was founded in 2021 by former OpenAI employees, including siblings Dario Amodei (CEO) and Daniela Amodei (President).

## Products & Services
- [[product:claude]] — flagship series of large language models, available via web interface, API, Amazon Bedrock, iOS app, and Mac/Windows desktop apps
- Claude Code — command-line AI agent for coding; reached general availability in May 2025 with VS Code, JetBrains IDE integrations, and GitHub Actions support
- Claude Cowork — graphical user interface equivalent of Claude Code, intended to be simpler to use
- Web Search API — launched May 2025; enables Claude to access real-time internet information
- Claude Gov — model for U.S. government/national security use, announced June 2025

## Corporate Structure
- Operates as a public benefit corporation (PBC)
- **Long-Term Benefit Trust**: A purpose trust for "the responsible development and maintenance of advanced AI for the long-term benefit of humanity." Holds Class T shares allowing it to elect directors to Anthropic's board. As of October 2025, members are Neil Buddy Shah, Kanika Bahl, Zach Robinson, and Richard Fontaine.
- **Labs division**: Introduced January 2026; Mike Krieger joined it
- **Anthropic Institute**: Think tank launched March 2026, led by Jack Clark, studying AI

## Competitive Position
- Direct competitor to [[company:openai]] in the LLM/AI assistant space; Anthropic was founded by former OpenAI employees
- Key differentiator: strong focus on AI safety; uses "constitutional AI" technique to fine-tune models
- Claude is the only AI model used in classified U.S. military missions (as of February 2026, via Palantir partnership)
- Competes with OpenAI, Google, xAI, and others for U.S. government/DoD contracts
- Amazon (primary cloud provider, $8B+ invested) and Google ($2.5B+ invested) are both major investors and distribution partners
- November 2025: Nvidia and Microsoft expected to invest up to $15 billion; Anthropic committed to buying $30 billion of compute from Microsoft Azure on Nvidia AI systems
- December 2025: $200 million multi-year partnership with Snowflake for enterprise AI deployments
- In contrast to OpenAI (which introduced ads to free ChatGPT), Anthropic stated Claude will remain ad-free
