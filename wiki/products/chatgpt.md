# ChatGPT

## Overview
ChatGPT is a conversational AI assistant developed by [[company:openai]], released in November 2022. Its launch has been credited with catalyzing widespread interest in generative AI. It is built on OpenAI's GPT family of large language models.

## Features
- Conversational AI interface powered by GPT large language models
- Extended capabilities via add-on products: ChatGPT agent, ChatGPT Atlas, ChatGPT Health
