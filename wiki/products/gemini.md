# Gemini

## Overview
Gemini is Google's family of large language models (LLMs), developed by [[company:google-deepmind]]. It represents Google DeepMind's primary generative AI product line and is part of a broader suite of generative AI tools developed by the lab.

## Features
- Large language model family (multiple variants implied)
- Developed by Google DeepMind alongside other generative AI tools: Veo (text-to-video), Imagen (text-to-image), and Lyria (text-to-music)
