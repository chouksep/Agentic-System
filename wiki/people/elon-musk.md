# Elon Musk

## Background
Elon Musk is one of the co-founders of OpenAI, which he co-founded in December 2015. He served as co-chair of OpenAI alongside Sam Altman at the organization's founding. He was motivated in part by concerns about AI safety and existential risk from artificial general intelligence. He was also an early investor in DeepMind Technologies prior to its acquisition by Google in 2014.
