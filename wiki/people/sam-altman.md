# Sam Altman

## Background
Sam Altman is one of the co-founders of OpenAI. He was motivated in part by concerns about AI safety and existential risk from artificial general intelligence when co-founding OpenAI in 2015. He expected OpenAI to be a decades-long project that eventually surpasses human intelligence.

## Current Role
CEO of [[company:openai]], a position he holds as of the source date. He is also a member of the founding group and was an original co-chair of the organization alongside Elon Musk.

## Previous Roles
- **Co-chair of OpenAI** (2015, at founding) — served alongside Elon Musk as co-chair at OpenAI's founding in December 2015
- **CEO of OpenAI** — removed by the board in November 2023 citing lack of confidence, then reinstated five days later following a reconstruction of the board
