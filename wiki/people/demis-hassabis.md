# Demis Hassabis

## Background
Demis Hassabis is a British AI researcher and entrepreneur. He met co-founder Shane Legg at the Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit at University College London (UCL). He co-founded DeepMind Technologies in November 2010 with Shane Legg and Mustafa Suleyman, with the stated goal of creating a general-purpose AI useful and effective for almost anything. The company described its early approach as teaching AI to play old video games from the 1970s and 1980s (including Breakout, Pong, and Space Invaders) without prior knowledge of the rules.

## Current Role
CEO of [[company:google-deepmind]]. Listed as a key person and co-founder of the company.

## Previous Roles
- **Co-founder**, DeepMind Technologies (November 2010) — founded the company alongside Shane Legg and Mustafa Suleyman

## Public Statements
- Described DeepMind's early AI approach: the company taught AI to play old video games from the 1970s and 1980s, one game at a time, without prior knowledge of the rules; "The cognitive processes which the AI goes through are said to be very like those of a human who had never seen the game would use to understand and attempt to master it."
- Stated the founders' goal is to create a general-purpose AI that can be useful and effective for almost anything.
