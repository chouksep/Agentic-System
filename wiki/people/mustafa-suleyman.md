# Mustafa Suleyman

## Background
Mustafa Suleyman is a British AI entrepreneur and co-founder of DeepMind Technologies, which he founded in November 2010 alongside Demis Hassabis and Shane Legg. He was a key figure at DeepMind from its founding through December 2019.

## Current Role
EVP and CEO of Microsoft AI, a newly created consumer AI unit at Microsoft. Appointed to this role in March 2024.

## Previous Roles
- **Co-founder**, DeepMind Technologies (November 2010 – December 2019) — one of three original founders of DeepMind
- **Policy role at Google** (December 2019 – March 2024) — joined Google in a policy role after departing DeepMind
