# Greg Brockman

## Background
Greg Brockman is one of the co-founders of OpenAI. In the early days of founding OpenAI, he met with Yoshua Bengio — one of the "founding fathers" of deep learning — and drew up a list of great AI researchers, successfully hiring nine of them as OpenAI's first employees in December 2015. OpenAI was initially run from Brockman's living room.

## Current Role
President of [[company:openai]]. He is a co-founder of the organization.

## Previous Roles
- **Co-founder of OpenAI** (December 2015) — was among the original eleven founders; helped recruit the first employees
- **Early operations lead** — OpenAI was initially run from his living room
